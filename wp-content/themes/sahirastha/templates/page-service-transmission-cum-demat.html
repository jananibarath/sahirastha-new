Transmission-cum-Demat

This page helps visitors identify whether their case fits the Transmission-cum-Demat route and what records are usually needed to proceed.

When this issue usually appears

Cases usually involve old records, mismatch in claimant or KYC details, incomplete papers, or uncertainty about the correct institution-side process.

How SahiRastha supports

  • Case classification and route clarity.
  • Review of available records and likely blockers.
  • Practical support for documentation, filing flow, and follow-up.
