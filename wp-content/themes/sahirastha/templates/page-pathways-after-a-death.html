Pathway: After a Death

After a death, one family may need to handle shares, insurance, PF, and deposits at once. The first step is to map assets and entitlement route before filing.

  • Collect available papers (certificates, policy docs, passbooks, statements).
  • Confirm nominee status and legal-heir position.
  • Split workstream by asset type and institution.

Relevant services: Shares, Insurance, PF / MF / Bank.
