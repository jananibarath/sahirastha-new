Process

  1. Share the broad facts and available records.
  2. Identify asset type, blockers, and likely route.
  3. Assess document gaps, nomination/heir issues, and complexity.
  4. Clarify scope and fee logic before execution.
  5. Proceed with filing support, follow-up, and realistic next steps.

Timelines vary by institution-side response, record quality, and entitlement complexity.
