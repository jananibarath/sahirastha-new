WHAT THE WEBSITE MUST COMMUNICATE
The site should make clear that:
- many recovery cases are not simple form-filling exercises

- different assets follow different authorities, document standards, and processes
- nomination does not always settle legal ownership issues
- missing documents, old addresses, KYC gaps, bank changes, death of holder, and name mismatches create delays
- NRIs and families often face additional difficulty because they are managing from outside the original location or after a death

PLATFORM AND BUILD DIRECTION
- WordPress
- Gutenberg-first approach preferred
- page content should remain editable in WordPress
- use reusable block patterns where useful
- avoid hardcoding important editable content into templates
- hero media should remain shortcode-replaceable
- do not put important text inside images
- if visual ambition conflicts with editability, prefer editability

THEME PREFERENCE
- OceanWP is the preferred starting point because of its customizability
- however, if OceanWP creates friction for Gutenberg-first editing, maintainability, or layout control, recommend a better block-friendly alternative
- do not force OceanWP if it becomes the problem

PERFORMANCE AND MAINTENANCE RULES
- keep the build lightweight
- avoid plugin bloat
- prefer free plugins unless there is a real reason not to
- keep long-term maintenance simple
- prioritize desktop + tablet + mobile responsiveness
